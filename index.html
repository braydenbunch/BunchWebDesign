FAQ

Asked before
every project

Do I own the website when it's done?
Yes — completely. Code, domain, hosting, and content all live in accounts under your name. At handoff you get the repository transfer, domain auth codes, and hosting access. If we part ways, everything keeps working.
What happens after I pay?
You get a confirmation email within a few hours with a link to book your kickoff call and a short intake form. Your project starts on the next available slot — usually within a week.
Can I update the site myself later?
Yes. Grow and Scale include a simple editing setup for things you change often — hours, menus, photos — plus a walkthrough at handoff so you're never stuck waiting on a developer.
What if I already have a website?
Even better — we're redesigning, not starting from zero. I'll audit what's working, keep what is, and rebuild the rest. Your domain and email stay exactly as they are.
Are there monthly fees?
Not to me. You pay your own hosting and domain directly (typically $10–25/month total), which keeps you in control. Ongoing help is available — but optional, never required.
